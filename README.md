
Most bank marketing campaigns fail before they even launch. They fail not because of bad offers, but because they treat every customer the same. In this portfolio project, I applied K-Means clustering to the UCI Bank Marketing Dataset to show why granular customer segmentation matters. 

Choosing K=5 over the statistically optimal K=2, I identified 5 actionable personas, from Young Professionals with high housing loan uptake to Financially Unengaged Outliers with zero loan activity. Therefore, giving banks a clear roadmap for who to target, how, and with what products.
